Noodles with Egg and Peas

Description

A very cheap, yet hearty and filling meal made with spaghetti noodles, peas, and coooked egg.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Fresh or frozen peas
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil 1/3 a box of spaghetti noodles until nearly finished
  2. When nearly finished, crack 3 eggs and add them to the pot
  3. 1 minute before removing, add in the peas
  4. Use 1/3 stick of butter to evenly butter the noodles
  5. Dust with parmesan cheese, stir, and serve
