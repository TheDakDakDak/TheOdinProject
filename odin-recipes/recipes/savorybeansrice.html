Savory Beans and Rice

Description

Kielbasa with beans and rice is a savory and hearty dish, and it's healthy too with added celery and sweet peppers.

Ingredients

  • Kielbasa
  • Rice
  • Pinto beans
  • Stewed tomatoes
  • Celery
  • Bell peppers
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut up sausage and add to pot
  2. Cut up vegetables and add to pot
  3. Add beans to pot
  4. Add tomato sauce to pot
  5. Cook rice separately
  6. Wait until almost done and add pre-diced garlic, italian seasoning, and salt
  7. Wait until it cools and serve.
